About Sean's Hockey Gear Store

Welcome to Sean's Hockey Gear Store! We are a small family-run business located in Portland, Oregon. We are dedicated to providing hockey gear to players of all skill levels, all at affordable prices.

Our store offers a wide range of hockey equipment, from sticks to the full gear set. Whether you are just starting out or are an experienced player, we have something for everyone.

Although we are based in Portland, all our items are available to ship online, ensuring you can get your gear no matter where your skates hit the ice!

Thank you for choosing Sean's Hockey Gear Store. We look forward to serving you!

If you have any questions or concerns please reach us at help@seanshockey.com
